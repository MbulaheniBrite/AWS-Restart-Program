## üéØ Objectives

Upon successful completion of this lab, you will have developed a comprehensive solution for the customer's issue and gained hands-on experience with foundational AWS networking components.

* **Summarize the Customer Scenario:** Understand the customer's architectural needs and the nature of their current networking problem (detailed in the provided email/architecture).
* **VPC Infrastructure Creation:** Create and configure the essential networking components for a secure and routable environment:

    * **Internet Gateway (IGW)**
    * **Route Table**
    * **Security Group (SG)**
    * **Network Access Control List (NACL)**
* **Compute Deployment:** Launch and configure an **EC2 instance** within the new network environment.
* **Console Familiarity:** Become proficient in navigating and utilizing the AWS Management Console for networking tasks.
* **Solution Development:** Develop and implement a working network solution that resolves the customer's issue.
